INVISIBLE DEATH PROJECT
2025
waste plastic
Only a small portion of the plastic we use is recycled, while most ends up in landfills, the ocean, or is incinerated. In particular, microplastics threaten the survival of invisible marine life but we fail to recognize the severity of this issue. This project magnifies radiolarians, microorganisms that play a crucial role in regulating Earth’s climate by storing carbon as they sink to the ocean floor. Using household plastic waste, it emphasizes the water pollution caused by plastic and highlights the ecological importance of these organisms, along with the crisis they face.
