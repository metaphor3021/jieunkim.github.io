Everyone’s Aliens to Someone
2024

I’m always fascinated by the fact that the world we see—our perception of it—is not everything.

What I see may not appear the same to other creatures.
Then, can we really say what we’re seeing and what we know?

When I think about this, I feel like an unknown being living in an unfamiliar world.
