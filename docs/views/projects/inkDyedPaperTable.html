먹을 품은 종이 탁상 / INK-DYED PAPER TABLE
2025
98(W) × 53(H) × 40(D) cm
Newspaper dyed with ink, finished with oil & varnish
Using newspaper as the main material, this reinterprets the traditional Korean table Gyeongsang in a contemporary way. By borrowing the structural principle of plywood, layers of dyed paper were stacked to create a solid table. Dyed with varying densities of ink, the paper reveals natural variations in grain, contrasting with the uniform patterns of plywood.
