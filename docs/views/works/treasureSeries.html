TREASURE SERIES
2024
This project is inspired by the forms of Korea’s national treasure ceramics, which were both symbols of power and ideal artworks in themselves. Discarded paper, stripped of its utility and value, is reborn in the shape of these treasured artifacts, revealing the potential and possibilities inherent in abandoned materials.
